Shakshuka

Description

Eggs poached in a rich, spiced tomato sauce with onions, peppers, and garlic, often served with fresh bread for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red onions,chopped
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced
  • small bunch coriander, stalks and leaves chopped separately
  • 2 cans cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 4 eggs

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan that has a lid, then soften the onions, chilli, garlic and coriander stalks for 5 mins until soft.
  2. Stir in the tomatoes and sugar, then bubble for 8-10 mins until thick.
  3. Using the back of a large spoon, make 4 dips in the sauce, then crack an egg into each one.
  4. Put a lid on the pan, then cook over a low heat for 6-8 mins, until the eggs are done to your liking.
  5. Scatter with the coriander leaves and serve with crusty bread.
