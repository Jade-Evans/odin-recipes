Vegemite and Avocado on Toast Recipe

Description

A classic Australian snack made by spreading a thin layer of Vegemite, a savory yeast extract, on buttered toast. Simple, salty, and full of umami, it's often enjoyed with a cup of tea or coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, peeled, sliced
  • 2 tsp vegemite
  • 4 slice sourdough
  • butter (for spreading)

Method

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Spread toast with butter and Vegemite.
  3. Top with avocado.
  4. Serve.
