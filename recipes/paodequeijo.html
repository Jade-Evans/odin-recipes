Pão De Queijo

Description

Chewy Brazilian cheese bread made with tapioca flour, giving it a unique texture and cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • oil or butter for the baking sheet
  • 250ml whole milk
  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 300g tapioca flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7. Oil or butter a baking sheet and set aside.
  2. Heat the milk, butter and salt in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from the heat and add the tapioca flour. Stir vigorously, then leave to cool a little.
  4. Pour the dough into a standing mix to beat the dough until its cool, or use a wooden spoon and beat by hand.
  5. Beat the eggs in one at a time, waiting until each is completely incorporated before adding the next.
  6. When the dough is glossy and mixed, add the parmesan. Beat again until mixed.
  7. Spoon the dough onto the baking sheet in 1 tbsp balls, 5cm apart.
  8. Bake for 30 mins until crisp and golden.
  9. Remove from the oven and eat while still warm.
